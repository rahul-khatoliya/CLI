About Us

Corporate Leasing India is an integrated services company specialising in real estate consulting and provides property solutions to multi-national corporations, large Indian companies and high net-worth individuals. Our corporate motto is to work for the best interests of our clients and thereby to build relationship of trust through commitment, integrity and honesty in our dealings. In the area of real estate development our services encompass identification of appropriate real estate options corresponding to your needs and cover land/estate negotiations and purchase. For leasing, we specialize in securing short and multi-year leases in renowned commercial real estate complexes at the most competitive prices. A highly professional team of negotiators and legal experts will assist you in finalizing the deal, drafting legally perfect lease agreements/contracts and in securing lease registration with the government registrar of property sales and leases. We aim to become a company that will be trusted by people to offer credible options to them when it comes to Real Estate. If you are looking for world-class realty solutions, please do not hesitate to approach us. Management Structure: An ISO 9001 Certified Establishment: We have a well defined and result oriented management system to achieve sustainable competitive advantage in the market place. Our team of experts works closely with the clients to understand their requirements and offer them the best deals which meet their budgetary planning and other concerns. The ISO certification is a testimony to our professional services that have established a new benchmark in the real estate sector.
